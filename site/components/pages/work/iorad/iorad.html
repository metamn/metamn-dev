Description

iorad is a startup helping businesses creating better tutorials on web and mobile.

Before the launch they've decided to give a face lift for their two most important pages: the Pricing and the Checkout page.

The project requirements were: speed of execution, keeping current visual assets untouched, cross-device and cross-browser compatibility. And a week duration from signing the contract to the final delivery.
