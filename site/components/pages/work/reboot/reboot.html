Description

Reboot is a social impact firm dedicated to inclusive development and accountable governance. They help governments, foundations, and international organizations achieve their missions.

After growing out the startup phase the Reboot design team created a new look and wanted a developer to implement in Wordpress.

The requirement for back–end was portability. No third-party plugins, just Wordpress core features and structures.
