Description

Scatter had ready a web application to connect local businesses with customers. They needed a website before the launch.

We've created a couple of landing pages describing product features both for businesses and customers. And a Pricing page to let visitors subscribe to their services.

On design the requirements were big typography with big background images, and intuitive graphics to explain product features. On development side we had to make everything fully responsible.
